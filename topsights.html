1.Lalbagh Botanical Garden

Lalbagh Botanical Garden, or shortened Lalbagh, is an old botanical garden in Bengaluru, India. First planned and laid out during the dalavaiship of Hyder Ali and later adorned with unique plant species by his son Tippu, it was later managed under numerous British Superintendents before Indian Independence.
Area: 97 ha
Founder: Hyder Ali
Metro: Bengaluru
District: Bengaluru Urban
