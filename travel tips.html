Financial Tips

Forex

  • While there’s no limit of cash visitors can bring into Karnataka, Indian customs may require you to declare any amount in excess of USD 10000 or equivalent.

  • All major cities have foreign exchange shops and airports have forex counters to convert your cash into local currency. Online services like bookmyforex can also be available Luxury hotels often accept payment in major foreign currencies.

  • Luxury hotels often accept payment in major foreign currencies.

Local Payments

  • Karnataka has strong network of Banks and ATMs- cash withdrawal can be done using your international visa/mastercard credit or debit cards (charges may apply). Bank branches operate six days a week. Timing and holidays may vary.
  • Most vendors accept electronic payments such as Google Pay, PayTM or credit/debit card payments. However carrying some cash is advised.

Timezone:India has only one time zone (IST) which is GMT + 5.30 hours.
